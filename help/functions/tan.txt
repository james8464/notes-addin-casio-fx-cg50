tan
Args: tan(expr)
Required: expr: angle; cos(expr) must be non-zero.
Optional: Exact special angles are simplified where possible.
Does: Returns tangent and supports trig solving/range routes.
Working: Rewrite to sin/cos or use tan identities for compound/double-angle work.
Checks: Domain excludes cos(expr)=0; bounded solve/range routes filter invalid points.
Examples: tan(pi/4)
