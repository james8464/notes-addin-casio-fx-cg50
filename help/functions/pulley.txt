pulley
Args: pulley(m1,m2[,g])
Required: Two masses m1,m2.
Optional: g, default 9.8.
Does: Finds acceleration and tension for two particles joined over a smooth pulley.
Working: Uses a=(m2-m1)g/(m1+m2), then KhiCAS simplification for T=m1(g+a).
Checks: Positive acceleration means m2 moves down.
Examples: pulley(2,3); pulley(2,3,g=10)
