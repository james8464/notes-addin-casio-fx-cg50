lcm(a,b,...)

Args:
  a,b,...

Required:
  a, b, ...

Optional:
  None.

Does:
  Least common multiple. Required: two integers or polynomials. Useful for algebraic denominators.

Examples:
  lcm(x^2-1,x^3-1)
