friction
Args: friction(mu,R[,F[,m]])
Required: Coefficient mu and normal reaction R.
Optional: Applied force F and mass m; named arguments mu=..., R=..., F=..., m=... may be used.
Does: Finds limiting friction, checks sliding, and finds acceleration if F and m are supplied.
Working: Uses Fmax = mu R, compares F with Fmax, then uses KhiCAS simplification for F-Fmax = ma when sliding.
Checks: For static friction use F <= mu R unless limiting friction is stated.
Examples: friction(0.4,20); friction(2/5,25,12); friction(2/5,25,12,4); friction(mu=2/5,R=20,F=6,m=2)
