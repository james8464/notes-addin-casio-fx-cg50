friction
Args: friction(mu,R)
Required: Coefficient mu and normal reaction R.
Optional: Named arguments mu=..., R=... may be used.
Does: Finds limiting friction.
Working: Uses Fmax = mu R and substitutes values.
Checks: For static friction use F <= mu R unless limiting friction is stated.
Examples: friction(0.4,20); friction(mu=2/5,R=20)
