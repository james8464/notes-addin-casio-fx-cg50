friction
Args: friction(mu,R[,F])
Required: Coefficient mu and normal reaction R.
Optional: Applied force F; named arguments mu=..., R=..., F=... may be used.
Does: Finds limiting friction and checks sliding if F is supplied.
Working: Uses Fmax = mu R, then compares F with Fmax.
Checks: For static friction use F <= mu R unless limiting friction is stated.
Examples: friction(0.4,20); friction(2/5,25,12); friction(mu=2/5,R=20,F=6)
