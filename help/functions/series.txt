Args: series(expr,var=value,order)
Required: expr, var=value, order
Does: Returns a Taylor expansion to the requested order.
Examples: series(sin(x),x=0,5); series(exp(x),x=0,4)
F2:series(sin(x),x=0,5)
F3:series(exp(x),x=0,4)
Ex F2: series(sin(x),x=0,5)
Ex F3: series(exp(x),x=0,4)
Working: Uses standard A-level expansion terms where routed.
