series
Args: series(f,var,a,n)
Required: f: expression; var: variable; a: centre; n: order.
Optional: Taylor-style syntax may overlap with taylor.
Does: Returns local series expansion.
Working: Use exact Taylor/series expansion around the centre to the requested order.
Checks: Series output is an approximation/truncated expansion, not full equivalence away from the centre.
Examples: series(exp(x),x,0,4)
