varacc
Args: varacc(a,t[,v0])
Required: Acceleration a in terms of t.
Optional: Initial velocity v0 at t=0.
Does: Integrates acceleration to velocity.
Working: Uses v=int a dt, then adds v0 if supplied.
Checks: For non-zero initial time, use integrate directly.
Examples: varacc(6*t,t); varacc(6*t,t,4)
