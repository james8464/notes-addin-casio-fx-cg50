varacc
Args: varacc(a,t[,v0[,s0]])
Required: Acceleration a in terms of t.
Optional: Initial velocity v0 and initial displacement s0 at t=0.
Does: Integrates acceleration to velocity, and then to displacement if s0 is supplied.
Working: Uses v=int a dt, then s=int v dt.
Checks: For non-zero initial time, use integrate directly.
Examples: varacc(6*t,t); varacc(6*t,t,4); varacc(6*t,t,4,2)
