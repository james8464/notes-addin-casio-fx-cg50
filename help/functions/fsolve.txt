fsolve
Args: fsolve(eq,var)
Required: eq: equation/expression; var: unknown.
Optional: Bounds fsolve(eq,var,a,b) may restrict the numeric search interval.
Does: Finds numerical solutions when exact solve is not suitable.
Working: Use exact simplification first, then numerical root finding on the active interval.
Checks: Numeric roots are substituted back into the equation and domain-checked.
Examples: fsolve(x^2-2,x)
