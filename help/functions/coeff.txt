coeff
Args: coeff(poly,var,n)
Required: poly: polynomial/expression; var: variable; n: requested power.
Optional: coeff(poly,var) may return coefficient list if KhiCAS accepts it.
Does: Extracts the coefficient of var^n after expansion/collection.
Working: Native KhiCAS output; no extra working lines.
Checks: Coefficient is checked by comparing the collected polynomial term with the original expansion.
Examples: coeff((2*x+1)^5,x,3)
