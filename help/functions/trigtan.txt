Args: trigtan(expr)
Required: expr
Does: Rewrites supported trig expressions using tan where useful.
Examples: trigtan(sin(x)/cos(x)); trigtan(sec(x)^2)
F2:trigtan(sin(x)/cos(x))
F3:trigtan(sec(x)^2)
Ex F2: trigtan(sin(x)/cos(x))
Ex F3: trigtan(sec(x)^2)
Working: Uses tan(x)=sin(x)/cos(x), sec^2(x)=1+tan^2(x), and related identities.
