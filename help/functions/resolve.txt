resolve
Args: resolve(F,theta)
Required: Force F and angle theta.
Optional: Named arguments F=..., theta=... may be used.
Does: Resolves a force into two perpendicular components.
Working: Gives F*cos(theta) and F*sin(theta) component lines.
Checks: Choose sine/cosine according to the angle shown in the diagram.
Examples: resolve(10,30); resolve(F=10,theta=30)
