work
Args: work(F,d)
Required: Force F in N and distance d in m in the direction of the force.
Optional: Named arguments F=..., d=... may be used.
Does: Finds work done.
Working: Uses W = Fd and substitutes values.
Checks: If force is at an angle, resolve the component in the direction of motion first.
Examples: work(12,5); work(F=12,d=5)
