resultant
Args: resultant(p,q,var)
Required: p,q: polynomials; var: eliminated variable.
Optional: None.
Does: Eliminates var by computing the polynomial resultant.
Working: Build resultant with respect to var after collecting both polynomials.
Checks: Output is checked against common-root elimination semantics where possible.
Examples: resultant(x^2-a,x-b,x)
