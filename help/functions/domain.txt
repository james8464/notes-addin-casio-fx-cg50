domain
Args: domain(expr,var)
Required: expr: expression; var: variable.
Optional: Predicates and intervals may be used by commands that call domain internally.
Does: Finds real input values for which expr is defined.
Working: Intersect restrictions from denominators, even roots, logs, inverse trig and trig poles.
Checks: Domain restrictions are reused by range, solve and xform checking.
Examples: domain(sqrt(ln((x+3)/(x-1))),x)
