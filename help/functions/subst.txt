subst
Args: subst(expr,var,value)
Required: expr: expression; var: variable; value: replacement.
Optional: subst(expr,var=val) may be accepted by KhiCAS syntax.
Does: Substitutes value for variable.
Working: Native KhiCAS output; no extra working lines.
Checks: Substitution result is checked by direct evaluation of the transformed expression.
Examples: subst(x^2,x,2)
