moment
Args: moment(F,d)
Required: Force F in N and perpendicular distance d in m.
Optional: Named arguments F=..., d=... may be used.
Does: Finds a moment about a point.
Working: Uses M = Fd, substitutes values, then gives the moment.
Checks: Uses perpendicular distance, not distance along a rod.
Examples: moment(12,3); moment(F=12,d=3)
