atan
Args: atan(expr)
Required: expr: real or symbolic expression.
Optional: atan(y,x) may be accepted by KhiCAS for quadrant-aware angle forms.
Does: Returns inverse tangent in principal range (-pi/2,pi/2).
Working: Use exact known angles first; otherwise keep exact inverse trig form.
Checks: For solve/range, periodic tangent solutions are interval-filtered when bounds are supplied.
Examples: atan(1)
