incline
Args: incline(m,theta)
Required: Mass m and slope angle theta.
Optional: Named arguments m=..., theta=... may be used.
Does: Gives standard weight components on an inclined plane.
Working: Gives down-slope component mg*sin(theta) and normal reaction mg*cos(theta).
Checks: Assumes no other perpendicular forces act.
Examples: incline(5,30); incline(m=5,theta=30)
