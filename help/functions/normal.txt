Args: normal(expr)
Required: expr
Does: Rewrites rational expressions over a common denominator and simplifies.
Examples: normal(1/(x+1)+1/(x-1)); normal((x^2-1)/(x-1))
F2:normal(1/(x+1)+1/(x-1))
F3:normal((x^2-1)/(x-1))
Ex F2: normal(1/(x+1)+1/(x-1))
Ex F3: normal((x^2-1)/(x-1))
Working: Shows denominator combination when routed.
