Args: trigcos(expr)
Required: expr
Does: Rewrites supported trig expressions using cos where useful.
Examples: trigcos(sin(x)^2); trigcos(tan(x)^2)
F2:trigcos(sin(x)^2)
F3:trigcos(tan(x)^2)
Ex F2: trigcos(sin(x)^2)
Ex F3: trigcos(tan(x)^2)
Working: Uses sin^2(x)+cos^2(x)=1 and reciprocal identities.
