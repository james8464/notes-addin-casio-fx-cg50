impulse
Args: impulse(m,u,v) or impulse(F,t)
Required: Mass m, initial velocity u, final velocity v; or force F and time t.
Optional: Named arguments F=..., t=... may be used.
Does: Finds impulse/change in momentum.
Working: Uses I = m(v-u) or I = Ft.
Checks: Sign shows direction from the chosen positive direction.
Examples: impulse(0.5,4,-2); impulse(F=12,t=1/2)
