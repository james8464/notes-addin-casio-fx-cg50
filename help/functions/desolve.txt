desolve
Args: desolve(ode,dep)
Required: ode: differential equation; dep: dependent function or variable.
Optional: Initial conditions may be supplied if accepted by KhiCAS syntax.
Does: Solves supported A-level differential equations exactly where possible.
Working: Identify separable/first-order linear form, separate or use integrating factor, then integrate.
Checks: Returned solution is checked by substitution into the differential equation where possible.
Examples: desolve(diff(y,x)=k*y,y)
