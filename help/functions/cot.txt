cot
Args: cot(expr)
Required: expr: angle; tan(expr) or sin(expr) must be non-zero.
Optional: None.
Does: Returns 1/tan(expr), equivalently cos(expr)/sin(expr).
Working: Rewrite to sin/cos or reciprocal tangent when simplifying identities.
Checks: Domain excludes sin(expr)=0; bounded solve/range routes filter invalid points.
Examples: cot(pi/4)
