Args: range(expr) or range(expr,var) or range(expr,var,a,b)
Required: expr
Does: Returns the range as inequalities in y.
Examples: range(-5*x^2+6*x+4,x); range(x/(1+x^2),x); range(sqrt(9-x^2),x); range(4*(x^2-2)*exp(-2*x),x)
