range
Args: range(expr,var)
Required: expr: function expression; var: variable.
Optional: range(expr,var,a,b) restricts to interval; range(expr,x>0) accepts simple predicates.
Does: Finds real output range.
Working: Intersect natural domain with constraints, split at poles/breakpoints, check endpoints, limits and stationary points.
Checks: Verified means the returned range was checked under the same domain/interval constraints.
Examples: range(ln(x),x,1,inf)
