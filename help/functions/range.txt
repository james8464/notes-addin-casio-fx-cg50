range(expr,[var,a,b])

Args:
  expr, [var,a,b]

Required:
  expr

Optional:
  variable and interval endpoints

Does:
  Returns the real range as inequalities where possible, including algebraic forms instead of only sampled values.

Examples:
  range(x^2); range(1/x)

F2:
  range(x^2)

F3:
  range(1/x)

Keys:
  F1 inserts the command.
  F2 inserts the first example.
  F3 inserts the second example when available.

Working:
  Finds restrictions, endpoints, turning points, asymptotes, and inequality form where routed.

Tip:
  Use brackets around compound numerators, denominators, powers, and substitutions for readable exam-style output.
