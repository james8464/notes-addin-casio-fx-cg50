Args: tcollect(expr)
Required: expr
Does: Collects trigonometric terms into a simpler supported form.
Examples: tcollect(sin(x)*cos(x)); tcollect(sin(x)^2+cos(x)^2)
F2:tcollect(sin(x)*cos(x))
F3:tcollect(sin(x)^2+cos(x)^2)
Ex F2: tcollect(sin(x)*cos(x))
Ex F3: tcollect(sin(x)^2+cos(x)^2)
Working: Uses A-level trig identities where routed.
