tcollect
Args: tcollect(expr)
Required: expr: trig/algebraic expression.
Optional: None.
Does: Collects trig expressions into compact identity-based forms.
Working: Use trig identities, common factors and algebraic collection.
Checks: Collected form is checked by expanding/simplifying back to the input.
Examples: tcollect(sin(x)^2+cos(x)^2)
