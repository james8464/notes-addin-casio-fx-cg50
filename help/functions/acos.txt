acos
Args: acos(expr)
Required: expr: value in [-1,1] for real output.
Optional: Use complex mode only if explicitly working outside real A-level routes.
Does: Returns inverse cosine in principal range [0,pi].
Working: Use exact known angles first; otherwise keep exact inverse trig form.
Checks: For solve/range, returned angles are filtered by domain and interval.
Examples: acos(1)
