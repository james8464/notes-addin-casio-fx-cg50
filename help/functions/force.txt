force
Args: force(m,a)
Required: Mass m in kg and acceleration a in m/s^2.
Optional: Named arguments m=..., a=... may be used.
Does: Finds resultant force using Newton's second law.
Working: Uses F = ma and substitutes values.
Checks: Force is in N. Direction follows the chosen positive direction.
Examples: force(12,3); force(m=12,a=3)
