sin
Args: sin(expr)
Required: expr: angle in radians unless calculator angle mode says otherwise.
Optional: Exact special angles are simplified where possible.
Does: Returns sine and participates in trig identities and equation solving.
Working: Use reciprocal/Pythagorean/double-angle rewrites through texpand/tcollect when useful.
Checks: Trig rewrites are equivalence-checked before being shown as verified steps.
Examples: sin(pi/6)
