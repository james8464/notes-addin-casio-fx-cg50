taylor
Args: taylor(f,var,a,n)
Required: f: expression; var: variable; a: centre; n: order.
Optional: None.
Does: Returns Taylor polynomial/series about a.
Working: Differentiate as needed and build terms up to requested order.
Checks: Taylor output is labelled as approximation/truncation unless exact polynomial equivalence holds.
Examples: taylor(sin(x),x,0,5)
