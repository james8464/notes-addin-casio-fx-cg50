suvat
Args: suvat(known=value,known=value,known=value)
Required: Any 3 named values from s,u,v,a,t.
Optional: Extra named values may be supplied. Aliases include distance/displacement for s, initial/start for u, final/end for v, acc/acceleration for a, and time for t.
Does: Uses KhiCAS solve to find obtainable constant-acceleration values and lists equations used.
Working: Solves v=u+at, s=ut+1/2at^2, v^2=u^2+2as and s=(u+v)t/2 exactly. Quadratic cases show paired solution lines and physical non-negative time branches.
Checks: Ignores unnamed/unknown inputs with a message. Contradictory inputs give No solution. Choose the positive direction first.
Examples: suvat(u=2,a=3,t=4); suvat(s=10,u=2,a=3); suvat(displacement=10,initial=2,acc=3); suvat(u=2,a=3)
