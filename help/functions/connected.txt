connected
Args: connected(m1,m2,F)
Required: Masses m1,m2 and external force F.
Optional: None.
Does: Finds acceleration and tension for two connected particles on a smooth line.
Working: Treats both particles as one system, then uses KhiCAS simplification for T=m1*a.
Checks: Force is assumed to pull the two-particle system with m1 behind.
Examples: connected(2,3,10)
