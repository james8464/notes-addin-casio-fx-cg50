Args: product(expr,var,start,end)
Required: expr, var, start, end
Does: Returns the product of expr as var runs from start to end.
Examples: product(k,k,1,4); product(2,k,1,n)

F3:product(2,k,1,n)
Ex F2: product(k,k,1,4)
Ex F3: product(2,k,1,n)
Working: Usually final-answer only; expands finite products when small.
