limit
Args: limit(f,var,a)
Required: f: expression; var: variable; a: approach value.
Optional: One-sided direction may be accepted by KhiCAS syntax.
Does: Computes exact limit.
Working: Simplify first, then use algebraic cancellation, standard limits and one-sided checks where needed.
Checks: Candidate limit is checked against KhiCAS exact limit and domain behaviour.
Examples: limit(sin(x)/x,x,0)
