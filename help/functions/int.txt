int
Args: int(f,var)
Required: f: integrand; var: integration variable.
Optional: Bounds int(f,var,a,b) for definite integrals if accepted.
Does: Alias for integrate.
Working: Use recognition, substitution, parts, partial fractions and trig rewrites as appropriate.
Checks: Antiderivative is checked by differentiating back to the integrand where possible.
Examples: int(cos(x),x)
