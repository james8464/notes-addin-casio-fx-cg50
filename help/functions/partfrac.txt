partfrac
Args: partfrac(expr,var)
Required: expr: rational expression; var: variable.
Optional: None.
Does: Decomposes rational expression into partial fractions.
Working: Factor denominator, divide first if improper, then solve constants for each factor.
Checks: Partial fraction form is checked by recombining to the original rational expression.
Examples: partfrac((2*x+3)/(x^2-1),x)
