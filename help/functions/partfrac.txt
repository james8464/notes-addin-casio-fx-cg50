Args: partfrac(expr,var)
Required: expr, var
Does: Splits a rational expression into real partial fractions.
Examples: partfrac((3*x+5)/(x^2+x-2),x)
F2:partfrac((3*x+5)/(x^2+x-2),x)
Ex F2: partfrac((3*x+5)/(x^2+x-2),x)
