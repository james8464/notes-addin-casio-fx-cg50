cosec
Args: cosec(expr)
Required: expr: angle; sin(expr) must be non-zero for real finite output.
Optional: Alias csc may not be visible; use cosec in this program.
Does: Returns 1/sin(expr).
Working: Rewrite to reciprocal sine when simplifying, solving or verifying identities.
Checks: Domain excludes sin(expr)=0; interval routes filter those singular points.
Examples: cosec(pi/2)
