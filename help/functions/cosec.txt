cosec(x)

Args:
  x

Required:
  angle x

Optional:
  None.

Does:
  A-level reciprocal sine notation. Internally equivalent to 1/sin(x); output may preserve cosec where exam-friendly.

Examples:
  xform(1+cot(x)^2,cosec(x)^2); diff(cosec(x),x)

F2:
  xform(1+cot(x)^2,cosec(x)^2)

F3:
  diff(cosec(x),x)

Keys:
  F1 inserts the command.
  F2 inserts the first example.
  F3 inserts the second example when available.

Working:
  Uses cosec(x)^2 = 1 + cot(x)^2 and reciprocal identities where routed.

Tip:
  Use brackets around compound numerators, denominators, powers, and substitutions for readable exam-style output.
