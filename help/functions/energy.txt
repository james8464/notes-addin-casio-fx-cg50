energy
Args: energy(m,v[,h]) or energy(m=,u=,v=)
Required: Mass m and speed v, or initial/final speeds u,v.
Optional: Height h for gravitational potential energy.
Does: Finds KE, GPE if h is given, or change in KE.
Working: Uses KE = 1/2mv^2, GPE = mgh, Delta KE = 1/2m(v^2-u^2), with KhiCAS simplification.
Checks: Uses g = 9.8. Units are joules.
Examples: energy(2,5); energy(2,5,3); energy(m=2,u=3,v=5)
