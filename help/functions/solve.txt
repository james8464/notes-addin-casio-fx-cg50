solve
Args: solve(eq,var)
Required: eq: equation/expression; var: unknown.
Optional: solve(eq,var,a,b) filters exact/periodic solutions to interval [a,b].
Does: Solves equations exactly where possible.
Working: Normalise equation, apply algebraic/trig/log/surd routes, then use KhiCAS exact solve as final engine.
Checks: All returned roots are substituted back and checked against domain and interval constraints.
Examples: solve(cos(x)=0,x,0,pi)
