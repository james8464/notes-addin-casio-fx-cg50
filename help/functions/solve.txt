solve(equation,var)

Args:
  equation/expression, var

Required:
  equation or expression, variable

Optional:
  None.

Does:
  Solves exact equations. Simple dy/dx separable differential equations are routed to A-level working before falling back to KhiCAS.

Examples:
  solve(x^2-5*x+6=0,x); solve((dy)/(dx)=y,y)

F2:
  solve(x^2-5*x+6=0,x)

F3:
  solve((dy)/(dx)=y,y)

Keys:
  F1 inserts the command.
  F2 inserts the first example.
  F3 inserts the second example when available.

Working:
  Collects terms, factorises, uses quadratic/rational/log/trig routes, and separates variables for supported first-order differential equations.

Tip:
  Use brackets around compound numerators, denominators, powers, and substitutions for readable exam-style output.
