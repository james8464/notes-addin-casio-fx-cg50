Args: solve(equation,var[,independent_var])
Required: equation, var
Optional: independent_var for simple separable differential equations
Does: Solves supported A-level equations over the real domain.
Examples: solve(x^2-5*x+6=0,x); solve(log(2,x)=3,x); solve(dn/dt=k*n,n,t)
