rewrite
Args: rewrite(expr)
Required: expr: expression to normalise.
Optional: Target form may be accepted by KhiCAS in some syntaxes.
Does: Rewrites expression into an equivalent simpler or standard form.
Working: Try algebraic, log, trig, reciprocal and power rewrites, then simplify.
Checks: Displayed rewrites must simplify back to the same expression under active assumptions.
Examples: rewrite(sin(x)^2+cos(x)^2)
