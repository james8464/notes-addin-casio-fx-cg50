integrate(expr,var,[a,b],[method],[u=...])

Args:
  expr, var, [a,b], [method], [u=...]

Required:
  expr, var

Optional:
  bounds a,b; method code; substitution u=...

Does:
  Finds antiderivatives or definite integrals. Method code guides the shown exam route: 1 auto, 2 by parts, 3 substitution, 4 partial fractions, 5 trig/formula.

Examples:
  integrate((ln(x))^2,x,2); integrate((ln(x))^2/x,x,3,u=ln(x))

F2:
  integrate((ln(x))^2,x,2)

F3:
  integrate((ln(x))^2/x,x,3,u=ln(x))

Keys:
  F1 inserts the command.
  F2 inserts the first example.
  F3 inserts the second example when available.

Working:
  Shows term-by-term integration, reverse chain rule, repeated integration by parts, substitution with user u, trig formulae, partial fractions, and limit substitution where routed.

Tip:
  Use brackets around compound numerators, denominators, powers, and substitutions for readable exam-style output.
