integrate
Args: integrate(f,var)
Required: f: integrand; var: integration variable.
Optional: Bounds integrate(f,var,a,b) for definite integrals; method hints may be accepted by KhiCAS.
Does: Integrates exactly where possible.
Working: Use recognition, substitution, parts, partial fractions and trig identities; show substitution lines for simple u-substitution.
Checks: Antiderivative is checked by differentiating back; definite results check endpoints/domain.
Examples: integrate(exp(x)/(1+exp(x)),x)
