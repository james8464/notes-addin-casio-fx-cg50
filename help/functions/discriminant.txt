Args: discriminant(poly,var)
Required: poly, var
Does: Returns the discriminant of a polynomial in var.
Examples: discriminant(x^2+x+1,x); discriminant(a*x^2+b*x+c,x)
F2:discriminant(x^2+x+1,x)
F3:discriminant(a*x^2+b*x+c,x)
Ex F2: discriminant(x^2+x+1,x)
Ex F3: discriminant(a*x^2+b*x+c,x)
Working: For quadratics, uses b^2-4ac and interprets real roots when routed.
