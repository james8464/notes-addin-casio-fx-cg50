projectile
Args: projectile(u,theta[,t])
Required: Initial speed u and launch angle theta.
Optional: Time t may be supplied for position at a time.
Does: Gives projectile setup lines for Paper 3 mechanics.
Working: Resolves into ux = u*cos(theta), uy = u*sin(theta), then gives time/range/height or x,y at time t.
Checks: Assumes no air resistance and vertical acceleration g downwards.
Examples: projectile(20,30); projectile(20,30,2)
