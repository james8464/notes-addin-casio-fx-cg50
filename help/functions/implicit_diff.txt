implicit_diff
Args: implicit_diff(eq,x,y)
Required: eq: relation in x,y; x: independent variable; y: dependent variable.
Optional: None.
Does: Finds dy/dx from an implicit equation.
Working: Differentiate both sides with respect to x, apply chain rule to y terms, then solve for dy/dx.
Checks: Result is checked by substituting into the differentiated relation.
Examples: implicit_diff(x^3+y^3=3*x*y,x,y)
