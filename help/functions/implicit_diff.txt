Args: implicit_diff(equation,x,y)
Required: equation
Does: Finds dy/dx for an implicit equation.
Examples: implicit_diff((x^2)*tan(y)=9,x,y)
F2:implicit_diff((x^2)*tan(y)=9,x,y)
Ex F2: implicit_diff((x^2)*tan(y)=9,x,y)
Working: Shows differentiating both sides, collecting dy/dx terms, and substitution.
