sum
Args: sum(expr,var,a,b)
Required: expr: term; var: index; a,b: integer bounds.
Optional: Symbolic/infinite forms may be accepted by KhiCAS if supported.
Does: Computes finite sum.
Working: Substitute integer index values over the bounds and add, simplifying exactly.
Checks: Bounds and index substitutions are checked for finite integer range.
Examples: sum(k,k,1,4)
