diff
Args: diff(f,var)
Required: f: expression; var: differentiation variable.
Optional: Higher derivatives may use diff(f,var,n) if accepted by KhiCAS.
Does: Differentiates using standard exact calculus rules.
Working: Use constant, power, product, quotient and chain rules; preserve grouping in quotient steps.
Checks: Differentiate result is checked by simplifying against the KhiCAS exact derivative.
Examples: diff(7*exp(x)/(sqrt(exp(x))-2),x)
