Args: diff(expr,var), diff(equation,var), or diff([x=...,y=...],t)
Required: expr/equation, var
Does: Differentiates with respect to var. Equation input uses implicit differentiation; parametric input gives dy/dx.
Examples: diff(x^3,x); diff((x^2)*tan(y)=9,x); diff([x=t^2,y=t^3],t)
Working: Shows product, chain, implicit, parametric, and rearrangement lines where routed.
