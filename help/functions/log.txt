log(base,x) or log(x)

Args:
  base, x

Required:
  x; base when not base 10/default form

Optional:
  base may be omitted for calculator default log form

Does:
  Works with logarithms to different bases and shows change of base for non-default bases.

Examples:
  log(2,x); solve(log(3,x)=4,x)

F2:
  log(2,x)

F3:
  solve(log(3,x)=4,x)

Keys:
  F1 inserts the command.
  F2 inserts the first example.
  F3 inserts the second example when available.

Working:
  Shows ln(x)/ln(base), log laws, domain x>0, and exponentiation when solving.

Tip:
  Use brackets around compound numerators, denominators, powers, and substitutions for readable exam-style output.
