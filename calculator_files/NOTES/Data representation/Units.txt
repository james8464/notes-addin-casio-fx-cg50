# Units

## Names and Symbols for Powers of 2

- powers of 2 are used for binary-based storage/memory sizes
- kibi:
  - symbol = ki
  - power = 2^10
- mebi:
  - symbol = mi
  - power = 2^20
- gibi:
  - symbol = gi
  - power = 2^30
- tebi:
  - symbol = ti
  - power = 2^40

## Names and Symbols for Powers of 10

- powers of 10 are used for decimal-based quantities
- kilo:
  - symbol = k
  - power = 10^3
- mega:
  - symbol = m
  - power = 10^6
- giga:
  - symbol = g
  - power = 10^9
- tera:
  - symbol = t
  - power = 10^12

## Key Difference

- powers of 2 are based on repeated multiplication by 2
- powers of 10 are based on repeated multiplication by 10
- kibi is not the same as kilo
- mebi is not the same as mega
- binary prefixes are more precise when describing computer memory/storage

## Useful Acronym

- brilliant = byte
- katie = kibi/kilo
- managed = mebi/mega
- gardening = gibi/giga
- tasks = tebi/tera
- perfectly = pebi/peta
- exciting = exbi/exa
